The importance of responsive design and its impact on the target audience

Responsive Designs for web based content is important because it enables websites to adapt to a user's device with being easier to navigate and use. Responsive Design benefits developers by mitigating time and cost consumption. a Responsive Design removes device and screen boundaries of accessable websites, instead of developers creating various additonal stand-alone sites. Google Analytics:
Provides a wealth of data for free to help assess the success of websites

Three different kinds of reports that will tell you what information your target audience is accessing:Back to top

  1. Audience Report: Information about the website visitors
  2. Behavior Flow Report: Navigation through the websites
  3. Leverage Site Search Report: What visitors search for on the website

Information about the website visitors

Audience Reports Provides information such as visitors age, gender, location, language, interests, and engagement. This reports allows for creation of personas, or fictional representations of ideal customers, based on attributes and needs. See which pages customers visit, how long they stay, how often they return, and how likely they are to convert. Knowing how your audience interact with your website, and how they respond to your content is necessary to build competitive advantages. Back to top

Navigation through the websites

Behavior Flow Reoprts shows how visitors navigate through your website from the first page to the last page. This report alls for the visualization of a users journey through the site, or the path visitors take used to achieve their goals. The report also identifies problem points, or obstacles in the site preventing visitors from accomplishing their tasks. Issues like confusing navigation, irrelevant content, or technical issues are recognized in the behavior flow report. Back to top

What visitors search for on the website

Leverage Site Search Report: can be used to detect your audiences most interested topics, inquiries, or problems. The report provides audience insight expressed through their own words. This report also measures the effectiveness of site search functionality, and its influence on user behavior and conversions. Back to top
