Describe how search engines work:

Search engines allow users to search the internet for content using keywords. They have algorithms that determine which web page is ranked before the other, giving users the best available experience be providing the searched for content as quickly as possible. Three steps for search engines include crawling, indexing and ranking. However, if building for users, not search engines, then the three queries to consider are 'Do'-transactional queries, 'know'-informational queries, and 'go'-navigation quieries

Explain importance of keywords and descriptions as meta tags

Meta Tags impact how a site appears in the search engine results page (SERPs) and the amount of people inclined to click through the website. Tags and descriptions help people understand what a web page is about, and the information helps people determine if the site is relevant to their search query.

Four Element that comprise writing content relevant links

The four elements for writing content relevant links are the foundations for SEO, which allow developers to better organize the optimization process.
  1. Technical SEO: How well content can be indexed
  2. Content: The most relevant/best response to user quieries
  3. On-site SEO: Optimization of content and HTML
  4. Off-site SEO: Authority built to increase trust and ranking

What constitutes a good heading heiarchy and include one example with more than one heading tag

Heading hierarchy is the relationship between main headings and subheadings. The structure shows users how topics fit together. These structures are important because it improves readability and user experience on websites. The h1 representing the most important idea on the page, and sub-sections organized with h2 level headings
